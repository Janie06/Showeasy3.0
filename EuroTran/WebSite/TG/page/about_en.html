About G-Yi

 

G-Yi Logistics was founded in 2016 as an affiliate of Eurotran Expo Service Co., Ltd., a vibrant professional group with plentiful experience performing logistics and on-site services for international expositions. We have served nearly a hundred well-known domestic and international expositions, including the Taipei International Machine Tool Show (TIMTOS), Taipei Cycle, COMPUTEX TAIPEI, the Kunshan Electronics and Electrical Equipment Show (KE Show), the Maanshan Electronics Expo, and the Changsha Electronics Expo, providing comprehensive on-site services such as forklift, exhibit unpacking and positioning, storage, transport, and customs clearance.

 

G-Yi's headquarters is located in Taipei City's Nangang District (near the Taipei Nangang Exhibition Center), with a service center in Shanghai and partners located around the world. Our dedicated, integrated local service network gives exhibitors convenient, quality, and secure one-stop, comprehensive exhibit logistics services.
