Our Services

 

G-Yi's comprehensive, one-stop exhibition transport system provides exhibitors with complete on-site services, including: forklift, exhibit unpacking and positioning, storage, customs clearance, exhibit display, and dismantling. Customers don’t need to worry about any third-party assistance or storage, cutting down on manpower and communication costs.

 

  • Forklift Services

     

    We provide professional forklift transportation services, including removing exhibits from containers/trucks before the show, transporting them on-site, and replacing them on containers/trucks after the show is over.

  • Unpacking and Positioning Services

     

    Our experienced on-site team can unpack your exhibits in the shortest time possible, whether you use wooden, metal or special packaging, and get them precisely to your exact display area.

  • Storage Services

     

    During shows, we can provide storage for your empty crates, and offer exclusive access to bonded storage facilities at the Taipei World Trade Center or Taipei Nangang Exhibition Center for the temporary storage of high-value or regulated items.

  • Transportation Services

     

    We provide international surface, air, and domestic door-to-door transportation.

  • Customs Clearance Services

     

    We provide professional customs clearance services to give exhibitors hassle-free peace of mind.
