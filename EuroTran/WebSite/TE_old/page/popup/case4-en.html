Visitors of sports and leisure fairs are mostly consumers and end-users, and exhibition goods are usually available for sale at the fair. This type of trade fairs which sell goods to the end-users have become increasingly popular with the general public.

Eurotran has accumulated abundant experience in handling the total international logistics operation and transportation for clients to join these "fair and sale" events. Participants of such fairs generally come from all over the world, and visitors like to see and buy exhibit goods shipped from numerous foreign countries. Eurotran is honored to enable our clients to reach more buyers at such fairs and contribute to the increase of world trade.

Eurotran communicates early with our clients for logistics preparation and outlines major groups of items for the client to easily display their products, whether for showing or selling purposes. Eurotran also closely monitors the process of safeguarding and moving the exhibit items in and out of the fair ground to ensure a successful exhibit.
