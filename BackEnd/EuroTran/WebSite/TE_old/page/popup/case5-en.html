Due to the diverse development of the world's finished goods, the manufacturing industry often sees linkages among the supply chains of various industries. Machinery shows exhibit large sized machines of high value, high unit prices, and high operating risk. By exchanging information of respective technologies, participants find ways to partner with each other to create closer manufacturing ties for the enhancement of global trade and economy.

As mentioned, machinery exhibited at such shows are high-end manufacturing tools that are highly priced, large in size, and heavy. Eurotran provides tailor-made processes of safe transportation for such machines. Eurotran 1) Studies the structure of the machine, 2) Measures its size and notes their unique characteristics, and 3) Prepares customized containers and related supporting tools to secure the machine inside the container so as to ensure its safety during transportation.

Since most exhibited items are extremely large, the process of packaging, loading, transportation, customs declaration, and other processes all require more manpower. Eurotran's total logistics and transportation solution ensures close attention to the execution of every step. If any delay is encountered, Eurotran's engineers are on site to address the bottlenecks promptly.
