International machine tool shows reveal the current trends in R&D, innovation, manufacturing techniques, and application technologies for the machinery industry. People coming from related sub-industries, research institutes, and colleges of technology also attend the shows to exchange views in seminars held by sponsors.

Eurotran provides a total logistics and transportation solution for our clients to display their products in the fair. We also act as a bridge between our clients and related government authorities such as the customs office, trade board, and others. Eurotran 1) Contacts and registers our clients to the fair authority, 2) Lists all related exhibited machines, 3) Declares and clears foreign customs, 4) Transports all the machine tools to the fair ground and fixes them in the right positions. These procedures are highly complex and no checkpoints are neglected. Eurotran ensures that every exhibited machine tool reaches their designated points without error.

In handling large sized machine tools, Eurotran's technicians, though they all possess rich experience, would still discuss in detail with the client to ensure that the machines are safely and punctually delivered to and positioned in the fair ground. Furthermore, Eurotran's engineers will precisely measure the weight of each machine and find its center of gravity and hanging points to ensure safe moving and transportation.
