Trade Shows

The Utmost Exhibition Platform to facilitate Cross-Strait Economic Development between Taiwan and China.

“Taiwan Trade Fairs in China”, organized by Taitra in cooperation with various provincial administrative of China, is the utmost important exhibition platform in facilitate cross-strait economic ties between Taiwan and China. Eurotran has been the logistics contractor of said Fairs ever since its launch in 2009, showcasing products of over ten-thousand exhibitors, touring 28 major cities in China over the years.

Dedication to enhance global exposure of Taiwanese Brands.

Eurotran’s dedicated team and reliable service earned a high-degree of recognition among industries while assisting Taitra to carry out the “Image Enhancement Project (IEP)” - a project which had already promoted 244 Taiwanese local brands, and facilitated 394 Taiwan companies to enter overseas markets.

Problem-solving flexibility developed from in-depths experience.

Taiwan Trade Fair tours various cities in China each year, and the dedicated service team dispatched by Eurotran has to follow the tour to ensure perfect logistics every step of the way. The main obstacle lies in the diversity of regional policies, customs clearance processes, regulations and certifications, and that’s where Eurotran’s years of rich experience in customs clearance and transportation kicked in, integrating and corresponding to such differences, providing problem-solving flexibility and ensuring delivery on a secure and timely manner.
