Events & Performances Logistics

Engaged in various international performances and events.

Eurotran expanded its business lines to the transportation of facilities and equipment for large-scale stage performances in 2009, and has worked with various international performance groups, such as Phantom of the Opera, Mamma Mia, Takarazuka Revue, Rent, etc.

Adequate Communications and planning brings the best out of performances.

In comparison to the transportation of exhibition goods, performance equipment handling has an even tighter time constraint. Taking into consideration the move-in and out schedule and unique geography of different performance venues, at the same time overseeing the customs clearance process so the equipment is delivered in time for move-in and its next touring stop is a challenge for detail planning. Eurotrans’ capability in planning and communication ensures precision in delivery of the facilities, and real time client support.
